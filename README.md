# Switch-Off Pong

![alt text](<assets/img/Switch-off Pong Cover.png>)

A twist on the classic arcade game. In Switch-Off Pong, the rules are the same, but the arena's light isn't reliable. When the light flickers and dies, the ball disappears in the dark. Rely on your instincts and anticipate the trajectory to win!

## Deploy on Itch.io
Go try the game here! : [Itch.io](https://spykielucilius.itch.io/switch-off-pong)

## Features
* **Dynamic Lighting:** The arena light randomly turns off and on, creating sudden moments of complete blindness.
* **Local Multiplayer:** Play against a friend on the same keyboard.
* **VS Computer:** Play against the computer.

## Controls
* **Player 1:** `W` (Up) / `S` (Down)
* **Player 2:** `Up Arrow` (Up) / `Down Arrow` (Down)

## How to Play
1. Deflect the ball past your opponent's paddle.
2. When the blackout happens, don't panic: estimate the bounce and keep moving.
3. First to 5 points wins!
